Refund & Cancellation Policy for Bookseller.com

1. Order Cancellation
- Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement if they have not yet been shipped.
- Once an order is shipped, it cannot be cancelled.

2. Returns & Refunds
- Damaged or incorrect products are eligible for return or exchange.
- To request a return, contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the product and packaging.
- Once verified, we will initiate a replacement or a full refund.
- Refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within 10-15 business days.

3. Non-Returnable Items
- E-books, digital content, and downloadable files are non-returnable and non-refundable.
- Items marked as “Final Sale” are also non-refundable.

4. Change of Mind
- If you no longer want the product but it has been delivered in good condition, refunds may not be granted unless the product is unopened and returned within 7 days. Return shipping will be borne by the customer.

5. Shipping Issues
- If an order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery window due to our fault, we may offer a refund or reshipment.
- We are not responsible for delivery delays caused by courier partners or incorrect address details provided by the customer.

6. Policy Updates
Bookseller.com reserves the right to change this policy at any time. The policy in effect at the time of your order will apply.
